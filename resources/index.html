Shares Making Yearly Highs

Site displays how many times a share on the ASX has made a yearly high in a week. The idea is that a share that is making new highs on multiple days is trending strongly and should be researched further. I created this as I got tired of looking at the data each day and trying to manually track with shares were coming up from day to day.

The data is scraped daily from the SMH website and aggregated.
